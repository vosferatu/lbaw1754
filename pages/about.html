About

Nowadays there are few applications where censorship is absent and users can share their thoughts/ideas, their work, and their interests freely. Currently available websites don’t allow the users to manage the content displayed nor the advertisements.

Our goal is to build a platform where users can post their news and get recognition by rewarding them with site exposure. The idea is that we intend to give users freedom in posting/deleting, commenting, upvoting/downvoting and tagging posts.
