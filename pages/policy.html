Ditto Content Policy

Guidelines

In order to get a healthy community, these are the guidelines to prevent misconduct within it:

  • Content is prohibited if it is illegal.
  • Content is prohibited if it is involuntary pornography.
  • Content is prohibited if it is sexual or suggestive content involving minors.
  • Content is prohibited if it encourages or incites violence.
  • Content is prohibited if it threatens, harasses, or bullies or encourages others to do so.
  • Content is prohibited if it is personal and confidential information.
  • Content is prohibited if it is spam.

If any user disrespects any of these rules, sanctions may apply, varying from temporary ban to permanent ban.

Privacy and Data

Ditto stores all information in well-protected web server and follows strict rules to prevent leaks. We do not sell information to third-party players.

All content is protected by the Copyright law.

If a user wants to delete its account all personal information is deleted but its posts, comments and votes are stored and can be accessed publicity.
